Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

We understand that you may have questions about how Resume AI works, how your data is handled, and what features the platform offers. Below are answers to the most common questions to help you understand the system clearly.

1. What is Resume AI?

Resume AI is a Django-based web application that allows users to build resumes from scratch using simple inputs as well as analyze existing resumes using Artificial Intelligence. It helps improve clarity, structure, and overall resume quality.

2. Can I create a resume without uploading one?

Yes. Resume AI allows users to generate a complete resume from scratch by entering basic details such as personal information, skills, education, and experience. No existing resume is required.

3. What file formats can I upload?

Resume AI currently supports:

  • PDF files (.pdf)
  • Word documents (.docx)

Other formats such as images or plain text files are not supported. PDF format is recommended for best accuracy.

4. Is my data safe?

Yes. Uploaded resumes and user inputs are processed only for generating results and are not permanently stored. The platform does not share user data with third parties.

For more information, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

5. How accurate is the AI feedback?

The AI provides suggestions based on standard resume best practices and common industry expectations. The feedback is intended as guidance and should be reviewed and adjusted according to individual career goals and experience.

6. Can Resume AI guarantee job placement?

No. Resume AI does not guarantee interviews or job offers. It is a support tool designed to improve resume quality, which may increase the chances of making a strong impression.

7. Do I need to create an account?

No account is required at this stage. Users can directly build or analyze resumes without registration. Account-based features such as saving resumes and tracking history may be added in future updates.

8. How long does resume generation or analysis take?

Resume generation and analysis typically complete within a few seconds. Processing time may vary depending on server load and file size.

9. Can I use Resume AI on mobile devices?

Yes. Resume AI is built using Bootstrap and is fully responsive, allowing smooth usage on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

10. Who developed Resume AI?

Resume AI is developed by Daiyan Ali Abbas along with team members as a practical project to demonstrate real-world integration of Django and Artificial Intelligence.

11. Will Resume AI remain free?

Currently, Resume AI is free to use as an educational and demonstration project. Additional advanced features may be introduced in the future, while core functionality will remain accessible.

12. Can I suggest improvements or report issues?

Yes. Feedback and feature suggestions are welcome. If you face any issues or have ideas for improvement, please reach out via the Contact Page.

13. What should I do if something goes wrong?

If you experience errors such as upload failures or incomplete output:

  • Ensure the file is in PDF or DOCX format.
  • Check that the file is not corrupted.
  • Refresh the page and try again.

If the issue continues, contact support for assistance.
