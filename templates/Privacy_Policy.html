Privacy Policy

At Resume AI, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use our platform to build or analyze resumes.

1. Information We Collect

  • User-Provided Information: Information you manually enter while creating a resume, such as name, skills, education, experience, and other resume-related details.
  • Uploaded Files: Resume files (PDF or DOCX) uploaded for analysis and feedback.
  • Basic Usage Data: Non-personal information such as browser type, device information, and general usage patterns to help improve the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Generating resumes based on user inputs.
  • Extracting and analyzing uploaded resume content.
  • Providing AI-based feedback and improvement suggestions.
  • Enhancing application performance, reliability, and user experience.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Resume AI does not permanently store uploaded resumes or generated resume data. Information is processed temporarily to generate results and is discarded after processing is complete, unless future features explicitly state otherwise.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Your data is never sold or shared for marketing purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

Resume AI may use third-party Artificial Intelligence APIs to analyze text or generate resume content. Only the minimum required data is sent to these services for processing, and they are not permitted to retain or reuse your data.

6. Your Rights

You retain full control over your data. Since resume data is processed temporarily, users may discontinue use at any time without concern of long-term data storage.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements or changes in functionality. Any updates will be posted on this page.
