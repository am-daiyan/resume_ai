Privacy Policy

At Resume AI, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our web application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Uploaded Files: Your resume (PDF/DOCX) is processed only for text extraction and analysis.
  • Basic Usage Data: We may collect non-personal data such as browser type, device, and usage statistics to improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information only for:

  • Extracting and analyzing your resume content.
  • Providing feedback and recommendations.
  • Improving the performance and usability of our platform.

3. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. Uploaded resumes are not shared with third parties and are only processed temporarily for analysis.

4. Third-Party Services

Resume AI may integrate with third-party AI APIs for text analysis. These services only receive necessary data to perform the analysis and do not retain your resume.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your uploaded data. Since resumes are processed temporarily, your files are not permanently stored on our servers.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
